About TwiDash

The #1 Fine-Grained Content Visualization and Analysis Tool

Most tools classified social media information into broad categories of hate speech or fake news. A more fine-grained classification is not provided.

TwiDash aims to classify data into fine-grained categories like authentic, fake, satire/parody, imposter, manipulated, hate speech, misleading content and more.

TwiDash provides a variety of visualizations of data using graphs, content dispersion in real-time using dispersion networks.

Motivation

Social media has resulted in vast amounts of information being created and propagated at unprecedented rates. Moreover, the format of social media content is easy to consume and share, giving it the potential to influence millions of people.


  • Techniques

    Training our tool on state of the art models and further improving upon them using real-time Twitter data.


  • Visualization

    Several tools have thus been developed to analyze and visualize information diffusion, fake news and hate speech. However, there is a lack of a comprehensive tool to analyze the diffusion of the different fine-grained types of information propagated on social media. TwiDash addresses this need.
